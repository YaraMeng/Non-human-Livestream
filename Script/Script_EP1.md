# Script\_EP1

## 🎥 01｜Livestream Crash \(0–5s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Shot: Handheld slight shake \(to create an unsteady, tense atmosphere\)

Fennie stands under livestream lights with slight sweat on her face\.

Background: LED lights flickering continuously with occasional screen frame stutters and freezes\.

Foreground: Dense translucent live stream bullet comments scrolling rapidly and overlapping on screen\.

### 🎧 Sound Design

Dense high\-frequency comment notification beeps

Digital glitch distortion sound

Faint electric current buzzing

### 🎼 Music

No music / Ultra\-low deep drone only \(strong oppressive atmosphere\)

### 🎭 Actions

Fennie blinks rapidly and nervously\.

Her hands unconsciously tug at the corner of her clothes\.

She forces a stiff smile that quickly fades away\.

---

## 🎥 02｜Backstage Damage Control \(5–10s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Cut to Johnny with low\-angle shooting angle\.

Cold red lighting fills the backstage environment\.

Multiple monitor screens display rapidly jumping data and numbers\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Fast and intensive keyboard typing sounds

Cold, crisp UI system prompt tones

### 🎼 Music

Add subtle continuous ticking beat \(tick tick tick\)

### 🎭 Actions

Johnny operates the screens quickly with his fingers\.

He never looks up, keeping his eyes fixed only on the monitors\.

### 🎤 Line \(cold and calm tone\)

“Cut the main camera\.”

---

## 🎥 03｜Public Humiliation \(10–15s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

The manager stands under strong harsh spotlight with oppressive composition\.

Fennie stands alone in the dark shadow area\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Ambient sound suddenly becomes dry and compressed

Slight indoor reverb echo

### 🎼 Music

One heavy low bass boom hit

### 🎭 Actions

The manager slowly hands over the document\.

Team members turn their heads and smirk quietly\.

### 🎤 Line

“You’re dragging the whole team down\.”

---

## 🎥 04｜Overwhelming Cyberbullying \(15–20s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Close\-up shot of a phone screen → negative online comments scroll rapidly nonstop\.

Comment text gradually enlarges and finally covers the entire screen\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Multiple overlapping whispers moving left and right in stereo

Fennie’s heavy and rapid breathing sound

### 🎼 Music

High\-frequency noise gradually builds up \(anxiety tension rising\)

### 🎭 Actions

Fennie’s hands tremble uncontrollably\.

---

## 🎥 05｜Emotional Breakdown \(20–25s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Long tracking shot in the empty corridor\.

Corridor lights turn off one by one with dynamic darkening lighting\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

High\-heel footsteps with empty corridor echo

Heartbeat sound gradually gets louder and heavier

### 🎼 Music

Continuous low bass, perfectly synced with heartbeat rhythm

### 🎭 Actions

She leans weakly against the wall\.

Her breathing is broken and unstable\.

Tears fall down but she makes no move to wipe them\.

---

## 🎥 06｜Dismissed and Expelled \(25–30s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Document handover scene in slow motion\.

Close\-up shot of trembling hands\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Amplified paper friction sound

All background sound suddenly cuts to complete silence

### 🎼 Music

All music stops completely \(creating silent vacuum feeling\)

### 🎭 Actions

She hesitates for half a second before taking the document\.

Her fingers grip the document tightly\.

---

## 🎥 07｜Leaving the Company \(30–35s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

She drags her suitcase and walks out of the company building\.

The company door slowly closes behind her\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Rhythmic rolling sound of suitcase wheels

Heavy loud door closing bang

### 🎼 Music

Sad and gentle piano melody starts to play

### 🎭 Actions

She wants to look back but stops herself\.

She finally chooses not to turn around again\.

---

## 🎥 08｜Despair on the Street \(35–40s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Night urban scene with cold neon color tone\.

Crowds blurred in the background while she stays completely still\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Distant city traffic ambient sound

Echoing residual sound of online hateful comments

### 🎼 Music

Piano melody plus ethereal background pad sound

### 🎭 Actions

She slowly crouches down on the street\.

She holds her knees tightly in her arms\.

---

## 🎥 09｜Time Loop Triggered \(40–45s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

The whole world visual distorts with fisheye stretch effect\.

Light and scenery continuously twist and warp\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Strong tape rewind sound

Time distortion rushing whoosh sound

### 🎼 Music

All music and sound reversed

### 🎭 Actions

She closes her eyes slowly\.

Her head leans slightly backward\.

---

## 🎥 10｜Rebirth \&amp; Comeback \(45–50s\)

### 🎬 Visuals

Practice room lights up with clean and bright light\.

Everything returns to normal and peaceful\.

### 🎧 Sound Effects

Audio snaps back to normal sharply

Normal human voice and ambient sound restored

### 🎼 Music

New hopeful rhythm restarts

### 🎭 Actions

She opens her eyes\.

Her eyes become completely cold, firm and determined\.

### 🎤 Line \(low voice, sharp and ruthless\)

“…I’m back\.”
